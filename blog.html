Discover Siquijor: The Island of Magic & Adventure

by Theresa Winston • Jan 1, 2025 at 4:30 pm • News

Siquijor is more than just an island—it’s an experience filled with mystery, beauty, and adventure. Known for its enchanting folklore, white-sand beaches, and breathtaking waterfalls, this island is a must-visit for travelers seeking both relaxation and exploration. Whether you’re diving into crystal-clear waters or chasing sunsets, Siquijor promises an unforgettable journey. Ready to explore?

Must-Visit Beaches in Siquijor

by June Terry • Jan 14, 2025 at 2:34 pm • News

If you love the beach, Siquijor is your paradise! Paliton Beach is perfect for sunset lovers, while Salagdoong Beach is ideal for thrill-seekers with its famous cliff diving spots. For a more secluded escape, head to Kagusuan Beach, a hidden gem with crystal-clear waters. Whichever you choose, Siquijor’s beaches offer a slice of tropical heaven!

Chase Waterfalls in Siquijor

by Jame Charles • Jan 15, 2025 at 1:23 pm • News

Siquijor is home to some of the most stunning waterfalls in the Philippines! Cambugahay Falls is the most famous, with three tiers of turquoise pools perfect for swimming and swinging from vines. Lugnason Falls is another beauty, offering a peaceful retreat in the heart of nature. A visit to Siquijor isn’t complete without chasing these natural wonders!

Explore Siquijor’s Mystical Side

by Lorena Winona • Jan 28, 2025 at 5:32 pm • News

Siquijor is known for its healers, legends, and ancient traditions. Visit the Old Enchanted Balete Tree, believed to be over 400 years old, and dip your feet in the natural fish spa. You can also explore the local healing practices and discover why Siquijor has long been called the Island of Magic.

A Foodie’s Guide to Siquijor

by Chester Ignacio • Feb 1, 2025 at 12:22 pm • News

Siquijor isn’t just about stunning landscapes—it also has delicious local flavors! Try Torta, a sweet sponge cake loved by locals, or enjoy a fresh seafood feast at one of the island’s beachside restaurants. Don’t miss out on coconut-based dishes that capture the island’s tropical vibe. Come hungry and leave happy!

Why Siquijor Should Be on Your Bucket List

by Hev Abisyo • Feb 3, 2025 at 3:52 pm • News

Looking for a travel destination that has pristine beaches, hidden waterfalls, rich history, and a touch of mystery? Siquijor is the place to be! Whether you’re an adventurer, a beach lover, or someone seeking peace and serenity, this island offers an experience like no other. Pack your bags and get ready for an island adventure you’ll never forget!
